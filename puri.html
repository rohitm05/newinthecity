  • The presiding deity of the temple is Goddess Mausima. The goddess is also revered as Mausima (the mother's sister) of Lord Jagannath. The word Mausima is an amalgamation of two words, Mausi and Ma. Mausi in Oriya language means Aunt and Ma means Goddess.

From the architectural point of view, the temple of Ramachandi is not important but from the religious point of view, it is one of the famous Sakta pithas of Orissa.
